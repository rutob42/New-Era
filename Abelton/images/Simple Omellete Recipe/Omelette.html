Simple Omelette Recipe


An easy and quick dish for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optioinal filings cheese, diced vegetables cooked meats, herbs
